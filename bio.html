Radhika Srinivas has a bachelors degree in Fine Arts from Stella Maris, Chennai, post graduate degree in textile design from The Fashion Institute of Technology, New York and a certificate in graphic design from The Parsons school of Design, New York. While living in the US, Radhika had pursued her career as a textile designer; her designs were featured in the International textile design shows at the Jacob Javitz Convention Center in New York. She continued to work there as a custom carpet designer at a well known custom carpet company, serving celebrity clientele.


She rediscovered her passion for watercolors while living in India for the past four years, and after a long hiatus, has resumed her art, mainly working in the medium of watercolors. She had a successful show in Chennai, India and has also done a lot work on commission for private homes. Her works have been chosen in Juried exhibitions, and her painting, New York, New York has won second prize and the board of directors awards in two different juried exhibitions.


Her source of inspiration comes from her various travels around the world, nature and scenes from everyday life.


She continues to discover herself by constantly experimenting with different techniques and it will be a never ending learning process. She currently lives in Devon, Pennsylvania and works in her art studio at home.
